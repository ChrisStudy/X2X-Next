## Overview

A cross-platform fitness tracking app focused on helping users build healthy habits through data-driven insights and engaging features.

## Key Features

- Guided workout sessions
- Nutrition tracking with barcode scanning
- Health progress analytics
- Wearable device integration
- Social and community-based challenges

## Tech Stack

Developed using **React Native** and **TypeScript**, with **Firebase** handling backend services and **Redux** managing application state.
